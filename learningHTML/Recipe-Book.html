Healthy French Fried Potatoes

Ingredients Quantity
russet potatoes
olive oil
rosemary
parsley
garlic salt		 1
1-2 Tablespoons
1 teaspoon
1 teaspoon
as desired

Directions: Preheat oven to 450 degrees. Wash and cut potatoes. Mix oil, rosemary and parsley into a bowl. Combine potatoes into seasoning mixture. Pour onto cookie sheet and sprinkle fries with garlic salt. Bake 15 minutes, turn potatoes over and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes.

Clam Chowder

Ingredients Quantity
potatoes
celery
onion
half & half
flour
butter
clams		 3-4
2-3 stocks
1/2
1 pint
1/2 cup & 2 Tbsp
1/2 cup
2 cans

Directions: Peel and cut potatoes, celery, and onion. Combine into pot, pour clam juice, and add water to barely fill. Cook veggies on a simmer for about 10-15 minutes. In separate pot, melt the butter. Slowly add the flour and then the half and half while whisking the chowder. Do this step slowly. Once the chowder is made, pour it into the vegetable pot, add clams, salt and pepper, and enjoy!
